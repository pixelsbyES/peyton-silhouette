Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Returns

Q: Do you really offer fast shipping on orders?

A: Yes! Our orders ship very quickly, and can be expected promptly.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: All sales are final and cannot be cancelled after the point of purchase. Due to the special nature of our merchandise, we cannot accommodate exchanges or refunds under any circumstances. In addition, our vendors are unable to cancel or make any changes or modifications to any order once it has been placed. Please be sure of the style, size, color, and any other specifications before submitting your order. Also, standardized dye lots cannot be guaranteed for orders with multiple dress styles.

Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: No. At this time we do not ship internationally. If you let us know that you are interested in ordering internationally we will try to accommodate on a case-by-case basis.

Q: How do I check the status of my order?

A: You will be notified via phone during various times during the ordering process. If you need additional information, contact a customer service representative by phone at + (234) 7061683705.

Payment Information

Q: Do you offer refunds?

A: Peyton Silhouette cannot refund any portion of your payment or accommodate any exchanges unless we have accepted the order as a return. Once your order has been submitted, our designers are unable to make any changes or modifications relative to style, size, color, or specific length on an order.

What forms of payment do you accept?

A: Peyton Silhouette currently accepts check payments, cash payments and credit payments including Visa, MasterCard, and Discover for all orders. The Bridal Boutique does not accept American Express at this time. The payment of your order is due in full at the point of purchase. We do not offer any payment or lay-away programs.
